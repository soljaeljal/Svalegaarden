What's our story?

Our history

In 1952 the beautiful merchant’s house was rebuilt on the corner of Thorsens Allé and Svalegårdsvej and it was named Svalegaarden. The idyllic building still stands on the same address and has been the host of many events and experiences throughout the years.

In 2016 we took over Svalegaarden, bringing new ideas, enthusiasm and hope for the future. We bring new energy and fresh eyes to the renovated merchant’s house.

Our vision is to take all our the best ideas and transfer them into the beautiful historical building of Svalegaarden, with its unique placement at the center of Hasseris, Aalborg.

- Mette and Stefan

Our team

We're a team of experienced professionals devoted to deliver an extraordinary experience.

Five chefs, two bakers accompanied by our waiters, we value highly the team spirit and the atmosphere we create for our guests.

Our traditional recipes are the heritage that we ultimately serve , however, with a modern twist.
